Oryza sativa), edible starchy cereal grain and the grass plant (family Poaceae) by which it is produced.

Roughly one-half of the world population, including virtually all of East and Southeast Asia, is wholly dependent upon rice as a staple food; 95 percent of the world's rice crop is eaten by humans.

    Requirements

  • Water
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Turmeric

    Steps

  1. Measure 1.5 cups of water for each cup of rice
  2. THrow it in a cooking pot for 15 minutes
  3. Throw in your Turmeric and Olive oil
  4. Enjoy!
